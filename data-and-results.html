CS 109 Project

About

This is the write-up for a Fall 2013 final project in CS 109 (Data Science) at Harvard University.

We examine where and when marine incidents happen on the east coast of the United States. We focus on:

  1. Visualization using tools such as GIS and Python's basemap package.
  2. Prediction using Poisson regression and machine learning algorithms.

Data

We use data about marine incidents from the United States Coast Guard. You can find out more about this data set here.

C. Hornbaker, S. Merity, I. Slavitt, 2013
